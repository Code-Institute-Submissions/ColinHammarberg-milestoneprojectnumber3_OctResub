LOVE THERAPY

Love Therapy is an application used and developed by (Improvement Therapy & Psychiatrist Centre). It was developed for old and new clients of the therapy centre and to offer them a more comfortable way of scheduling appointments, contacting their therapist, staying anonymous, having their bookings on a schedule and documenting about how they feel. The therapy industry hasn't been digitized on a worldwide basis yet. Most centres are not even offering digital meetings to their clients, which has made it difficult for people during the COVID-19 pandemic. The usual way of handling client bookings is to do it through email communication and that the client has to send the therapist/psychiatrist an email to inform them of a new session, especially government based support centres. Love Therapy offers full anonymity and does not require any accurate personal details, which gives some clients a more trustworthy relationship. The clients are also offered digital meetings, which is done through the platform SPCE. If you would like to read more about how SPCE works, then read here.
